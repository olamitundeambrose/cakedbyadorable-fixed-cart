# Custom Cake Builder - Add to Cart Requirements

## Required Selections (Must Complete All 6)

The "Add to Cart" button in the Custom Cake Builder section will only work after completing these **6 required selections**:

1. **Size** - Choose cake size:
   - 6" Round (serves 6-8 people) - €0
   - 8" Round (serves 10-12 people) - €25
   - 10" Round (serves 15-18 people) - €50

2. **Cake Type** - Choose cake shape:
   - Round Cake - €0
   - Square Cake - €5
   - Heart Shaped - €15
   - Number Cake - €20

3. **Flavor** - Choose cake flavor (from available options)

4. **Filling** - Choose cake filling (from available options)

5. **Delivery Option** - Choose delivery method

6. **Delivery Date** - Select a delivery date

## Optional Selections (Not Required)

These selections are optional and won't prevent adding to cart:
- Finish Color
- Design Accessories
- Description/Special Instructions
- Cupcakes
- Uploaded Image

## Button Behavior

- **Disabled State**: Button shows "Add to Cart" but is grayed out with message "Please complete all required selections"
- **Active State**: Once all 6 required fields are selected, button becomes clickable
- **Success**: Shows toast "Custom cake added to cart!" and updates cart count

## Base Price
- Starting price: €160
- Additional costs added based on selections
- Total price calculated and displayed in real-time
